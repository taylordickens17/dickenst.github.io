About Me

I was born and raised in Dallas, Texas and moved to Indianapolis around highschool. I was enrolled at Herron School of Art and Design studying Printmaking. Since graduating, I had a couple of odd jobs. I worked in an Adidas Warehouse printing t-shirts. It wasn't fullfilling working there. From there, I decided to make a career change. I took several semesters at Ivy Tech, taking a couple of courses about coding and the theory. The classes were informative and helpful, however, it felt too slow of a pace. A year later I enrolled at 1150 Academy to learn basic javascript and persue a career in tech.

Ghibli API

This is a Ghibli API. It generates every movie from Studio Ghibli by pressing a button. There is a blossom animation effect over the layout. Once generated, the movies are displayed in a container that is scrollable.
